About Saylani Welfare

Saylani Welfare International Trust

Saylani Welfare International Trust is a non-profit organization based in Pakistan that is primarily focused on providing various forms of social welfare and community development services.

Founded in 1999 by Mr. Arif Lakhani, Saylani has grown to become one of the most prominent and respected charitable organizations in Pakistan.

Mission and Objectives

Saylani Welfare's primary mission is to alleviate poverty, provide education, healthcare, and vocational training to underprivileged communities in Pakistan. The organization aims to improve the living standards and overall well-being of the marginalized and disadvantaged population.

Services and Programs

  • Food Distribution: One of the most well-known initiatives of Saylani is its massive food distribution program. They provide free meals to the needy on a daily basis.
  • Vocational Training: Saylani offers vocational training programs to equip individuals with practical skills that can help them secure employment and become self-sufficient.
  • Healthcare Services: The organization runs free medical camps and clinics, providing healthcare services to those who cannot afford them.
  • Education: Saylani supports educational initiatives, including schools and scholarships, to promote literacy and education among disadvantaged communities.
  • Orphanages: They operate orphanages and provide care and education to orphaned children.

Funding and Donations

Saylani relies on donations from individuals, corporations, and philanthropic organizations to fund its various projects and initiatives. They are known for their transparent financial practices and efficient use of funds.

Recognition and Awards

Over the years, Saylani Welfare International Trust has received recognition and awards for its humanitarian efforts and contributions to society.

Impact

The organization has had a significant impact on the lives of countless individuals and families by providing them with access to basic necessities, education, and vocational training.
